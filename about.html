PG Labs is the consultancy arm of PeaceGeeks. We help nonprofits and funders understand and employ AI and emerging technology safely, ethically, and strategically.

Our Story

A Decade of

Humanitarian Tech.

For over 10 years, PeaceGeeks has built digital tools that have reached 400,000+ people in crisis. We've seen where technology succeeds—and where it creates risk.

PG Labs translates this real-world experience into strategic advisory. We bridge the gap between "Tech Hype" and "Humanitarian Values," ensuring your organization stays innovative without compromising safety.
