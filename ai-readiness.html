Responsible AI Readiness, Risk & Ethical Use Program (RAIRRE), is a complete readiness program to help social impact organizations and funders mitigate risk, establish clear AI policies, and train staff on responsible use.

The Hidden Risks

AI is already in
your organization.
Is it governed?

Your organization is already using AI—with or without formal policies. Shadow AI, unvetted tools, and undocumented workflows create invisible risks: data breaches, ethical blind spots, and inconsistent outcomes that can undermine your mission and erode donor trust.

Intentional Implementation

Move beyond ad-hoc experimentation. Defining clear, approved AI use cases that directly advance your mission and solve real operational bottlenecks.

Ethical & Safe Usage

Protect the communities you serve. Implementing robust frameworks to mitigate algorithmic bias, secure sensitive data, and uphold your core values.

Consistent Governance

Eliminate staff guesswork. Establishing plain-language policies and uniform training so your entire team operates under shared, reliable standards.

— The RAIRRE Methodology —

A Proven Framework for Responsible AI.

A systematic approach to transition from unmanaged AI use to a funder-compliant and mission-aligned standard.

Phase 1

Organization-Wide Assessment

Role-based surveys to generate an AI maturity score and a priority risk heat map across your entire organization.

Phase 2

Role-Based AI Literacy

Calibrated training for frontline, management, admin, and technical staff covering ethics, bias, and when not to use AI.

Phase 3

Ethical Use-Case Enablement

Practical, approved workflows with “Approved / Conditional / Prohibited” guidance and human-in-the-loop checklists.

Phase 4

Governance & Sustainability

Plain-language AI policies, RACI matrices, and incident response protocols to ensure long-term, safe AI adoption.

RESULT

Future-Ready Organization

Lead with confidence using a custom roadmap and board-approved governance framework.
